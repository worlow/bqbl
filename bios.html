Hailing from Iowa, Jimmy Hans has served as the commissioner of the BQBL for three seasons now. Under his leadership the league has seen tremendous change, evolving from the primitive Grantland site, to the Google Docs version, and eventually to a league-owned website.

Work Wyroance - Sworls

Sworls was the leader of the committee that was responsible for migrating the BQBL to Google Docs after Grantland discontinued it. A former winner of the Best Student Open, he uses his background in mathematics and computer science to maintain the organization of the league and also write efficient, well-styled code for the website.
